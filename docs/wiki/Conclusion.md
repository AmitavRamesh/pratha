# Conclusion

## Summary of Work
We have successfully built **PRATHA**, a multimodal AI application that empowers consumers with health and sustainability data. By integrating real-time database lookups with advanced LLM-based analysis, the project offers a robust solution for interpreting product labels.

## Key Learnings
*   The effectiveness of **Multimodal LLMs** in replacing legacy OCR pipelines.
*   The importance of standardized schemas (Pydantic) in ensuring reliability between AI outputs and web interfaces.
*   The growing critical need for transparency in the global food supply chain.

## Final Outcome
The final result is a production-grade documentation and a functional prototype that demonstrates the feasibility of AI-driven personal health and environmental assistance.
