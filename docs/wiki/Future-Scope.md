# Future Scope

## Possible Improvements
*   **Offline Mode**: Implementing a lightweight local model (like MediaPipe) for basic barcode and OCR tasks.
*   **User Profiles**: Allowing users to save their scan history and set dietary goals (e.g., "Low Carb").
*   **Community Contributions**: Enabling users to upload corrected data to Open Food Facts directly from the app.

## Extensions
*   **Marketplace Integration**: Suggestions for sustainable alternatives available in nearby stores.
*   **Social Sharing**: Sharing "Green Score" or health achievements on social platforms.
*   **AR Overlay**: Using Augmented Reality to overlay Nutri-Score data on the physical product in real-time.

## Research Opportunities
*   Developing more efficient visual-tokenization for nutrition labels.
*   Studying the long-term impact of AI-driven nudges on sustainable consumer behavior.
