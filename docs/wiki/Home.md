## Short Description
PRATHA is a multimodal AI application designed to promote healthy consumption and sustainable living. By leveraging computer vision and Large Language Models, it enables users to scan food products via barcodes or direct media (images/videos) to receive instant nutritional insights, eco-scores, and tailored health warnings.

## Problem Statement
In today's fast-paced world, consumers often struggle to understand complex nutritional labels and the environmental impact of the products they purchase. Existing solutions are either limited to simple barcode scanning or lack deep analysis of ingredients and sustainability metrics, leading to uninformed choices that affect both health and the planet.

## Objectives
*   To develop a multimodal scanning system using Google Gemini AI.
*   To integrate Open Food Facts API for real-time product data retrieval.
*   To provide an intuitive dashboard for health and sustainability metrics.
*   To offer actionable sustainability tips and personalized health warnings.

## Key Features
*   **Barcode Scanning**: Instant lookup using the Open Food Facts database.
*   **Multimodal Analysis**: Upload images or videos of product labels for AI-driven extraction.
*   **Health Warnings**: Real-time detection of high sodium, sugar, or allergens.
*   **Eco-Score Tracking**: Visualization of the environmental impact of products.
*   **Sustainability Tips**: Practical advice on recycling and upcycling packaging.
